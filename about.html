Our Story

Trust, integrity & long-term value.

Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, Fauzan International serves importers, wholesalers, distributors, food processors, retailers and government procurement agencies across global markets.

We work closely with trusted farmers, processors, rice mills, packers and logistics partners to ensure consistent quality and uninterrupted supply. Our expertise in international trade enables us to understand market requirements and deliver products that meet global standards.

  • Our MissionSupply premium Indian agricultural products to international markets while creating long-term value for our customers through quality, reliability and trust.
  • Our VisionTo become one of the most trusted global export companies representing the excellence of Indian agriculture worldwide.
